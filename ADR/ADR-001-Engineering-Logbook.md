# ADR-001

## Title

Establish a Permanent Engineering Logbook

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Cougar OS is expected to become a large multi-year engineering project.

Without documentation, valuable engineering knowledge and architectural reasoning would be lost over time.

---

## Decision

Create a permanent Engineering Logbook that documents every meaningful engineering session.

Major engineering decisions will also receive Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).

---

## Consequences

Positive

- Engineering knowledge becomes permanent.
- Design decisions remain traceable.
- Progress becomes measurable.
- New contributors can understand historical reasoning.
- Demonstrates engineering maturity.

Negative

- Requires additional documentation effort after each development session.
